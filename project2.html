Helping students

2D Illustrations,
brand identity, strategy and animation

My colleagues and I worked on a project intended to help university students. In particular, those who are alone and subject to psychological illnesses due to stress and anxiety. We wanted them to find a space where to breathe. A spot both soft comfortable, like a pillow—”cuscino” in Italian. The project collaborates with various entities, implementing three solutions. A series of seminars conducted by experts; the formation of support groups; group activities, generating a community that fights stress through entertainment.

Given the delicacy of the subject, we decided to take advantage of a strategy that has already been proved valid by several web artists. It mixes an ironic language with a relatable one; this led us to create several illustrations.

We even made a 30ish second animation serving as the project promotional video.

While the animation was made using the grease pencil tool on Blender, the sound design and mixing were made with VEGAS Pro 14.

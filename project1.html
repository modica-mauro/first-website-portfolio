Merging 3D and 2D

2D illustration,
3D sketching

After my first year at “Design e Comunicazione“ ended, I reviewed it on an Instagram post. While the post description had the review, the image was an illustration that I drew which described and somewhat summed up the mood and the overall experience that the first year left me with.

Blender and other 3D softwares were used to get a correct and realistic perspective, while also evaluating framing and composition.

I put easter eggs here and there to mention the most interesting stuff happened:
—A Spray bottle made out of fabric, wood and plastic, for the modelling class.
—A bear-shaped blancket! A concept for the “concept design” class.
—Suzanne (the monkey’s head) for the Blender class.
—The graphic project “Componi la tua vita” about Componibili Kartell.
—A rhino’s head, for the Rhino class.

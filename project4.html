Making a soap run

3D Animation

For our 3D animation class, we had to come up with a 60 seconds story about sustainable packaging. The product we chose for our story was Jonna Breitenhuber’s “Project SOAPBOTTLE.” SOAPBOTTLE is a packaging for personal care and hygiene products made from soap. Once the liquid content is finished, the empty container can be used as hand soap. In our story, Soapy is a SOAPBOTTLE packaging participating in a race against other packagings.

You can see the thought process and evolution of the character design through our first sketches and concepts.

Here we were developing Soapy’s design and Petro’s design—the main antagonist.

While still working on the environment and the various rigs, we were starting to adapt our script in various storyboards and animatics—changing the story and the script along the way, cutting the unnecessary and improving scenes.

Blender was used for the overall animation. For editing, sound design/mixing, and compositing, VEGAS Pro 14 was used instead.
